Privacy Policy

VAT Calculator Extension  ·  Last updated: March 2026

This extension does not collect, store, transmit or share any personal data whatsoever.

Overview

The VAT Calculator browser extension ("the Extension") is a free tool published by FeelClean and Doors Delivered. It allows users to quickly add or remove UK VAT at standard, reduced or zero rates.

Data Collection

The Extension collects no user data. Specifically:

No personally identifiable information, financial data, location data, browsing history, web activity, or any other information is collected, logged, stored, or transmitted — at any time, for any purpose.

How the Extension Works

All calculations are performed entirely within your browser using standard JavaScript arithmetic. The numbers you enter never leave your device. The Extension does not make any network requests and does not communicate with any external server.

Permissions

The Extension requests no browser permissions. It operates solely on values entered directly by the user within the extension popup.

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with, or send data to, any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Because no data is collected, this Extension is safe for users of all ages.
